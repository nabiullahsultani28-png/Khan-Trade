CRT Curriculum

Seven modules. Full coverage.

Foundations first, execution last, the personal secret on top. Each module is a self-contained block — you can always come back to one specific piece without re-watching everything.

Module 01

CRT Foundations & Narrative

What Candle Range Theory actually is — the underlying delivery model, why a range gets formed, and the narrative every candle tells you if you know how to read it. This module rewires how you look at a chart.

  • The CRT delivery model
  • Range high / range low narrative
  • How CRT differs from generic ICT
  • The "why" behind each candle

Outcome: read any chart without indicators and know which candle matters.

Module 02

Timing & Session Alignment

CRT is a session-based model. You'll learn which kill-zones produce real CRT and which look like CRT but don't deliver. London Open, NY AM, the macro windows — each one mapped.

  • London Open kill-zone (02:00–05:00 NY)
  • NY AM session (08:30–11:00 NY)
  • Silver bullet windows (10–11 AM & 2–3 PM NY)
  • When to step away from the screen

Outcome: stop trading random hours. Start trading where CRT actually fires.

Module 03

Purging Candle Delivery

The purging candle is the engine of CRT. This module teaches you to spot it forming in real time, distinguish a true purge from a fake-out, and know the exact moment delivery is complete.

  • Anatomy of the purge candle
  • True purge vs. fake-out
  • Body / wick / close logic
  • How to read it live, not in hindsight

Outcome: identify the candle that pays you — before it closes.

Module 04

CRT Models & Confirmations

The four CRT variants and the confirmation each one requires. Removes the ambiguity between "this looks like CRT" and "this is a tradeable CRT with confirmation."

  • The four CRT variants
  • Per-variant confirmation rules
  • What invalidates a setup
  • Skip-or-take decision framework

Outcome: stop forcing trades that "kind of" qualify.

Module 05

Liquidity & Market Behavior

Where liquidity actually sits in Gold and the FX majors, how price hunts it, and how to read draw-on-liquidity bias to project the next move — not just explain the last one.

  • Buyside & sellside liquidity
  • External vs. internal liquidity
  • Draw-on-liquidity bias
  • Pre-positioning around news / sessions

Outcome: stop reacting. Start anticipating.

Module 06

Advanced CRT Execution

The mechanical layer that turns a good idea into a repeatable PnL — entry refinements, stop placement, partial-take rules, position sizing, and trade-management heuristics.

  • Entry refinement on lower timeframes
  • Stop placement logic (not just "above wick")
  • Partial / runner management
  • Position sizing & daily risk caps

Outcome: a checklist you can follow on autopilot.

Personal CRT Secret 🔑

The one filter Khan applies to every CRT entry that has shifted his accuracy more than any other refinement. Never taught in the free channel. Never shared publicly. Reserved for premium members.
