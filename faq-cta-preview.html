What's the difference between Free, Premium, and the full course?

Free Telegram covers the ICT and CRT basics, weekly commentary, and public setup breakdowns. Premium Telegram ($20/mo, $300 lifetime) adds 2–3 active signals a day with entry, stop and target on every call.

Full Course + Discord ($50/mo, $500 lifetime) is the complete thing: 4–5 daily signals, the full ICT, CRT and Unicorn curriculum, a private Discord, and direct time with the team. The Pricing page lays it out side by side.

Time02 / 05

How much time do I need each day?

Plan for 1–2 hours during the London or New York session. CRT is session-based — outside the kill-zones there's nothing to do but study.

Experience03 / 05

Do I need prior trading experience?

Helpful but not required. If you know candles, support/resistance, and basic risk management, you have enough. The free channel is the on-ramp — start there.

Feedback04 / 05

Can I ask questions and get feedback?

Yes, depending on tier. Premium Telegram is mostly signals. The real back-and-forth — posting your charts, talking to the team directly — lives in the private Discord that comes with the Full Course.
