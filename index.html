The Unicorn Model

Rare. Clean. Repeatable.

The Unicorn is the highest-conviction entry in the system — a breaker block overlapped with a fair-value gap, taken at a liquidity sweep, confirmed by timing. It doesn't appear every day, and that's the point.

01

Identify the breaker

The structure-defining order block that flipped polarity after liquidity was taken.

02

Overlap the FVG

Confirm the breaker overlaps a fresh fair-value gap on the entry timeframe.

03

Wait for the sweep

Price must take liquidity at the level before any entry is considered.

04

Confirm & execute

Lower-timeframe confirmation in the kill-zone, then a defined entry with stop and target.
