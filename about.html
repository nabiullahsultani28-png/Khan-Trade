Philosophy

Four principles. No exceptions.

Timing > everything

CRT only fires inside specific windows. Outside them, the best move is to stay flat.

One model, sharpened

Mastery of one entry beats dabbling in ten. CRT is the model — Unicorn is its edge case.

Checklist, not vibe

Every trade has a fixed checklist. If a row doesn't tick, you don't click — even if it "feels right."

Risk is the product

You don't trade for PnL — you trade for survival first, edge second. Risk caps are non-negotiable.

Who this is for

Serious traders. Period.

This isn't a content brand. It's a small, focused operation for people who treat trading as a craft, not a side-quest.

You belong here if…

  • You're building real trading skill, not just consuming content.
  • You're focused on Gold and the FX majors.
  • You can show up daily for the sessions.
  • You're ready to follow a checklist, not chase setups.

You don't belong here if…

  • You want copy-trade signals or "magic" indicators.
  • You're treating this as a get-rich-quick play.
  • You can't risk what you trade with.
  • You're not willing to study and screen-time.
